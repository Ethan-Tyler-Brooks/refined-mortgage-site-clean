My Story

It started with a mortgage that almost fell apart.

A few years ago, I was the first-time buyer. My wife and I had a pre-approval from our bank and were two weeks into buying a condo when my loan officer called to tell me I'd been denied. He felt terrible — but by then there was nothing he could do. That call is the reason I do this job.

A great lender stepped in, we closed on time, and I asked a thousand questions along the way. The more I learned, the more one thought kept nagging at me:

"I think I could do it better myself."

So I set out to. I became a mortgage advisor obsessed with the parts of the process that had failed me — clear communication, no surprises, and a plan that actually accounts for your life. Today I lead Refined Mortgage Group, an independent Wisconsin branch of Fairway, and I've had the privilege of helping over 1,000 families get to the closing table with their confidence intact.

Here's something I tell every client: you're not hiring a bank. You're hiring a person — a local advisor who knows this market, picks up the phone, and treats your loan like it's his own. That difference is the whole point.
