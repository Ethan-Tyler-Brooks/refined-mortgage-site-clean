My Story

Why I became an advisor.

Five years ago, I was a first-time homebuyer myself. I had a pre-approval from my bank and started shopping for a condo with my wife. Two weeks into the transaction, my loan officer called and told me I was denied a mortgage. He felt terrible — but there was nothing he could do.

My realtor referred me to their go-to lender, and the rest is history. We closed on time, and I asked endless questions about the process. The bug was planted. As I reconsidered my career, I kept returning to that stressful experience and thought: "I can do this better."

So I learned how to do it better. Today I've helped over 1,000 Wisconsin clients close on time or early, and built a community of raving fans and realtors who trust their clients with my team and me.
